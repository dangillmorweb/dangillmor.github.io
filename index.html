Thanks for stopping by…

This is what I consider my “anchor site” on the Web. Think of it as a portal to (almost) everything I’m doing, online and offline.

My primary gig these days is at the Walter Cronkite School of Journalism & Mass Communication at Arizona State University, where I work mostly on digital media literacy and entrepreneurship. I’m also a blogger, author, speaker, media investor and co-founder of several online businesses.
